About Me

Toni Anguiano

Hello all! My name is Toni and I am a mainly self-taught studio/digital artist based in the Bay Area, California. I recently graduated from San Jose State University where I obtained a BFA in Digital Media Art, which has become my primary medium as of these last few years. My work consists of various topics and themes, however I am most inspired by film, music, popculture, true crime, mythology and theology, to name a few. Although I create a lot of my work in Adobe Illustrator, and on paper, I am also highly skilled in the realm of 3D modeling and intermediate in 3d animation. I am a jack of all trades and I look forward to creating as much work as I can, strengthening my skill sets, and growing my portfolio!
